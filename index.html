▶ Tuning voice characteristics through PCA-based parameter adjustments

These audio samples demonstrate the impact of perturbing speaker embeddings along PCA-derived directions. Note that these perturbations affect both speaking style and voice timbre in the VITS (text-to-speech) system. In contrast, for the NANSY-VC (voice conversion) system, the changes primarily influence voice timbre. In addition, each principal direction in NANSY-VC predominantly affects only one or two perceptual voice attributes, such as pitch, vocal tension, brightness, and nasality. This focused impact significantly reduces the cognitive load on the listener during each iteration of the interactive refinement process.
